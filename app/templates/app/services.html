OUR SERVICES

Training on Investment Opportunities

We provide a breakdown of investment options available to individuals and groups by first analysing the capital requirement of each option and the risk profiles for all investment products. We effectively take our clients through products offered in the capital and money markets, train them on product description for stocks, bonds, derivatives, money market funds, SACCOs and how to invest in each one of them.

Financial Planning and Budgeting

Over and above the initial training of our clients, MK Consultancy will endeavour to walk the journey with our clients for a period to help actualize financial plans suitable to the clients. These would involve feedback sessions to assess the suitability of the set actions and/ or if they may need some adjustments.
